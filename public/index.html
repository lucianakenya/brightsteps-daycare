After-School Pickup

We offer safe and reliable after-school pickup for children enrolled in nearby schools. Our trained staff ensures a smooth transition from school to daycare, with snacks, homework help, and supervised play until pickup time.

  • Available Monday–Friday, 3:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Pickup from select local schools
  • Includes snack, quiet time, and structured activities
  • Optional extended care until 7:00 PM
